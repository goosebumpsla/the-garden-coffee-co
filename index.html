Bringing the garden to your event.

The Garden Coffee Cart Co. is a mobile coffee cart serving the greater Los Angeles area. Our garden-inspired coffee cart — featuring a La Marzocco espresso bar adorned with fresh flowers, ribbons, and botanical details — comes directly to your celebration. Every cup is crafted with care, every coffee cart setup designed to feel like an extension of your event's beauty.
